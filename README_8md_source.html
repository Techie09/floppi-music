1 Floppi-Music - Crazily advanced floppy music player for Raspberry Pi
2 ====================================================================
3 
4 The Floppi-Music project aims at creating a "crazily advanced" floppy
5 music framework for the Raspberry Pi, written in pure Python. The full
6 documentation is hosted at http://natureshadow.github.com/floppi-music .
7 
8 Motivation
9 ----------
10 
11 Everyone is doing floppy music these days, and it really gets old and
12 boring. However, Eike badly wanted to try floppy music himself, and of
13 course I wanted to meet his desire. Our plan was to create the floppy
14 music code in pure Python, and we wanted to use the Raspberry Pi to have
15 a lot of high-level interfaces available.
16 
17 The final goal is to build a "floppy music jukebox" for presentation at
18 events and conferences.
19 
20 Implementation / design goals
21 -----------------------------
22 
23 Our intention was to break loose from the low-level approach to floppy
24 music controller programming and use modern paradigms in a modern
25 language instead.
26 
27 The floppy music engine is entirely multi-threaded, with a playback
28 engine scheduling playback of notes for the various floppy drive
29 threads.
30 
31 The framework is designed so it can be easily extended or used as a
32 library. For example, all recognized input formats are parsed by
33 seperate functions and converted to an internal format suitable for
34 playback by the engine. It is easy to create such parsers for any other
35 (sensible) file format.

48 What works
49 ----------
50 
51  * Parsing of MML (Music Macro Language) files
52  * Playback of 4 (or 8) simultaneous tracks
53  * floppi-play command to play recognized, single files through the engine
54 
55 What is planned (Roadmap)
56 -------------------------
57 
58  * Parsing of MIDI files
59  * A webserver to select tracks and manage a playlist
60  * An mpd-compatible network interface
61  * Control through an LCD display and buttons on a controller
62  * Self-tuning of floppy drives
